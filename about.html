About me

Hi! I am Aryan. I'm a fourth year student at IIT Bombay, pursuing my Dual-Degree (Bachelor's and Master's) in Electrical Engineering with specialization in Microelectronics. Currently, I am also pursuing Minor degree in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay.

Electrical devices have always fascinated me. Billions of transistors on a nail sized silicon surface is just truly amazing. How computers could solve complex problems with just an arithmetic logic unit, which itself is an awesome circuit. I love to learn how different circuits work, debugging them and making different devices out of them which have practical application.

I love failures as they make me identify my weakness and shortcomings. Throughout my career, I have faced many challenges. Some lead to success and others to ultimate failure, but left me as a grown up man.

Student Satellite Team is a family where I enjoy working and helping my team to do an extraordinary job of building a satellite. I joined the team in '18 and is an active member since then, dreaming of making India as a space superpower.

I am die heart fan of Kishore kumar, sounds old?? but his mesmerizing voice would never get old :) I love playing cricket and football (not on PC !), and drawing Madhubani Painting, the famous artwork of Bihar.
IIT has taught me many things, apart from studies. It gave me new challenges, new friends and I am damn sure that when I pass out from here, I would definitely be a "Man".
